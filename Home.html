KOREAN FOOD

Korean food, or hansik, is known for its diverse dishes, fermented ingredients, and a balanced blend of animal and plant products. A typical Korean meal features rice, kimchi, soup, a main course (often a stew or grilled meat), and a variety of side dishes (banchan).
